Who are you?

By Ananya, listener from India

In bleak of past year desolation
howled silence laced back in ears
a creature none saw cease breath
who are you? ashes of dead stars

dying but slowly at a sunken sea
surprised cremation, unfortunate victims
fade to the final act of les Huguenots
who are you? a barren magic trick

thousand Irish goodbyes in a minute
dim glow roar of known loved one's
as orphans registered the hollow scar
who are you? the rebirth of hitler

end of our endings folded in an origami
we may live, love, laugh or perhaps die
on the lucifer's terms and conditions
who are you? a sinner of sin
