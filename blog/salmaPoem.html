Feathers in the Breeze

A poem by Salma, USA
Foes are near,

Friends feel far,

The world stands still like it’s stuck in tar

Loved ones are gone,

And the storm rages on,

Heroes zoom into the invisible enemy,

While we zoom out into reality,

But we’re feathers in the breeze,

So light,

We glide through the air with ease,

So light,

we drift in the night-

So light,

We escape the tar with might,

With this love and this ease,

We will beat this disease,

We work together,

As it takes,

As it hates,

We are together forever.

-Salma
