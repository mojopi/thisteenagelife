Inner Monologues

The Question...

  • How does the question "What do you want to be when you grow up?" make you feel?
  • Do you feel like people see kids only as "future adults?"
  • Do you think that there is a "what do you want to be when you grow up" anymore?
  • Who do you want to be when you grow up?
  • In addition to talking about the questions, your evolution of thinking about the topic, maybe we'll write letters to our future selves and make an episode about this pervasive question.

Comparisons

  • When it comes to comparing yourself to other people, along what dimenions do you compare yourself?
  • Are there big (e.g. frequent jockeying with a best friend) and incessant (e.g. comparisons to ads and social media) ways you compare yourself?
  • Why do you think you compare yourself? Is there ever a time when it's helpful for you?
  • How do you think comparisons affect your mental health? If they've been hard for you, how do you work with them?

Mental Health & Anxiety

  • What does your anxity sound like?
  • What does mental health mean to you? How has your mental health been during the pandemic?
  • How do you feel that your mental health is related to your relationship with your phone, technology, or other factors like getting to go outside, the weather etc?
  • What external factors shift your mental health and what are things that you do that how you that your mental health needs some TLC?

  • What are ways that you take care of yourself?

Rules of Engagement

  • Kindness and authenticity
  • Taking people's stories seriously and cherishing them
  • No mockery, demeaning vibes (i.e. eye rolling)
  • Only telling stories connected to you (i.e. don't challenge or correct other people's stories and use I statements)
  • What's said in this Zoom room stays here.
  • No recordings shared without permission and you don't want something to be recorded while you're recording, say that so that it is recorded
