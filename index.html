This Teenage Life is a podcast about the ideas and stories from teenagers in the middle of their own growth as humans as they have unique perspectives on the world around them. The episodes that you’ll hear, and the people that you’ll meet, are meant to be related to, laughed at, and most of all offer a connection to whoever is listening.

For us, it's also a way to process what we feel and think. We all have our experiences and sharing them with those you feel comfortable with makes the world a little less big, and a little more loved.

We hope that after listening to these stories you feel the same way. — Cloe Moreno
